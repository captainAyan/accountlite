AccountLite

Lightweight Double-Entry Accounting Software

About

AccountLite is a lightweight accounting software mainly focused towards Double-Entry accounting system. AccountLite DOES NOT have inventory management, point of sale or invoicing system. AccountLite has Journal, Ledger, Trial Balance statement, and Final Statement (Revenue & Expense Statement and Balance Sheet).

The data (your accounting data) is stored in a human readable format (which is very similar to CSV), on your own device. We don't store your any data (your accounting data) in our databases.
